## What is Physical AI?

**Physical AI** refers to AI systems that interact with the real world through physical bodies (robots), sensors, and actuators. Unlike traditional AI that operates purely in digital environments, Physical AI systems must:

- **Sense** their environment through cameras, IMUs, force sensors, and other modalities
- **Act** on the world through motors, grippers, and locomotion systems
- **Learn** from sensorimotor feedback in embodied contexts
- **Collaborate** with humans in shared physical spaces

This textbook focuses on **humanoid robots**‚Äîrobots designed with human-like forms‚Äîas a platform for understanding Physical AI principles that apply broadly across robotics.
